Rotherglen School Kindergarten Addition

Architect                      McCallum Sather Architects (MSA)
Partner-in-Charge                      Joanne McCallum
Project Architect                      Raminder Kanetkar
Year work completed                      2008
Structural Consultants                      Halcrow Yolles
Project Value                      $1,500,000
Project Location                      Oakville, Ontario

Project Description:
Gross Floor Area: approx. 7,900 sq ft
This was a complex project due to the site limitations and the building program involved. Parking and construction space were limited on site due to existing trees. The city of Oakville required a comprehensive tree inventory for the removal of existing trees for new building construction. Two (2) kindergarten classrooms were designed on the ground level with administrative offices on the 2nd level. The building was designed with a basement where ample storage for the kindergarten classrooms could be provided along with the mechanical and electrical rooms. The main design aim was to have a safe environment with ample light in the classrooms and other interior spaces. The central entry was through two storey atrium space, containing a feature stair and the reception desk, topped with skylights. All classrooms and administrative offices had clerestory windows, in addition to vision glazing, for glare free light. The exterior expression of the building was kept fairly traditional, designed to complement the existing school building.
The tender documents for this project were were finished, the site plan was approved and the building permit process was nearing completion, when the owner decided not to proceed with the construction due to kindergarten enrolment issues.

Project Involvement/Role:
Raminder Kanetkar as the Project Architect was responsible for the Site and Building design, Project Approvals from the Authorities, Code analysis, Contract Documents, and the Consultant Co-ordination.

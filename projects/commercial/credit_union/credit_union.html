Credit Union Office and Banking Space

Architect                      McCallum Sather Architects (MSA)
Partner-in-Charge                      Greg Sather
Project Architect                      Raminder Kanetkar
Year work completed                      2007
Project Value                      $1,500,000
Project Location                      Stoney Creek, Ontario

Project Description:
Gross Floor Area: approx. 3,500 sq ft on 1 floors
Credit Union was designed to accommodate a new breed of banking that was more open to public, had advanced security features, and embraced the sustainability guidelines. This project was featured on TV show "The Daily Planet" for its progressive design features. Although located in a suburban plaza surrounded by ample parking, the project was respectful of the pedestrian circulation in the plaza.

Unique Design Features:
Some of the project's unique design features included: A state of the art Electronic Vault that could identity user through the fingerprints eliminating the need for monitoring
Curved Roof Form - A distinguishable curved roof form that made the building stand out
Clerestory Windows for maximum Access to Natural Light - All open areas and enclosed rooms were located to have direct access to natural light through clerestory windows reducing glare and associated cost of blinds.
Green Roof - Lower Roof on both long sides of the building was intended as green roof.

Challenges Unique to the Project:

  • Building curved roof was challenging that required rigorous envelope details.
  • Interior structure to support the curved roof was chosen as glue lam beams and columns. Precise dimension and details were developed to achieve the roof curves.
  • Interior spaces were a combination of open and closed offices and public areas. An open plan was challenging for interior acoustics especially with the curved roof. In some spaces like washrooms, an interior roof had to be installed for acoustic and visual privacy.
Project Involvement/Role:
As a Project Manager and designer a significant responsibility of running the project from start to finish :
  • Material selection for interior & exterior
  • Exterior envelope design and details
  • Interior space design and details
  • Production and co-ordination of tender documents
  • Responsible for all consultant co-ordination including structure, M&E
  • Construction monitoring and shop drawings

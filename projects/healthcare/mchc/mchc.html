McMaster Children's Health Centre (MCHC) MCYS Funding Submission

Architect                      McCallum Sather Architects (MSA)
Partner-in-charge                      Joanne McCallum
Project Architect                      Raminder Kanetkar
Year work completed                      2008
Project Value                      $40,000,000
Project Location                      Hamilton, Ontario

Project Description:
Gross Floor Area: approx. 105,000 sqft on 4 floor
This was essentially a schematic design exercise/design study to create a building in compliance with the building program to secure funding from the Ministry of Children and Youth Services (MCYS). This is currently a P3 project for which building design and compliance teams have already been selected. The RFP is out for building financing and construction team to bid on from three shortlisted teams.

Unique Design Features:
The proposed design had various unique aspects in its exterior as well as interior design, some of which have been discussed below:

  • A unique glassed in dome as an igloo like atrium space housing the reception, cafeteria and other collaborative spaces at the rear of the building
  • A green roof
  • A colorful art glass wrap around corner window towards the front SE corner to help create the building presence
  • 9mx9m structural grid used to maximize flexibility for interchangeability of counseling and office spaces
  • Extra wide corridors
Challenges Unique to the Project:
This was a public private partnership (P3) project for which the critical part was to secure funding and approvals from Ministry of Children and Youth Services (MCYS). The project layout had to justify and be in compliance with the building program. Many layout options were developed and tested against the building program and finally the most fitting one was approved by the Hamilton Health Sciences (HHS). This rigorous design exercise required working closely with the Resource Planning Group (RPG), the programming firm, in understanding the needs of the unique users.

Project Involvement/Role:
From the project Inception to the MoHLTC submission stage involved in the capacity of Project Leader with:
  • Programming
  • Concept Design and Documentation
  • Code analysis
  • Level 4 submission report
  • Outline specifications
  • Co-ordination of drawings
  • Consultant co-ordination
